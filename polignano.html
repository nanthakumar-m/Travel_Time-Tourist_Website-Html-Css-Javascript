Polignano a Mare is a picturesque coastal town located in the Apulia region of southern Italy. Perched atop dramatic limestone cliffs that overlook the crystal-clear waters of the Adriatic Sea, this charming town is renowned for its stunning natural beauty and historic architecture. The town's whitewashed buildings, narrow winding streets, and quaint piazzas create a postcard-perfect Mediterranean atmosphere. One of Polignano's most famous attractions is the Grotta Palazzese, a unique restaurant situated inside a natural cave, offering diners a breathtaking view of the sea. Visitors can also enjoy sunbathing on the small beaches, exploring the ancient town center, and savoring delicious Italian cuisine in the local trattorias.
